Frequently Asked Questions

How do I add a new housing unit?
To add a new housing unit, navigate to the Housing section and click the "Add Housing" button. Fill in the required details about the unit including location, size, and amenities, then click "Save".
How can I generate QR codes?
Go to the QR Codes section and click "Generate QR". Select the location type and specific unit or area, then choose whether to download or print the generated QR code.
How do I send notifications to residents?
In the Notifications section, click "New Notification". You can send to all residents or select specific groups. Type your message, set the priority level, and click "Send".
